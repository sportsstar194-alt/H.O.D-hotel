About H.O.D

H.O.D (Haven of Distinction) is a globally renowned luxury hotel brand with headquarters in Delhi NCR, India. We've redefined hospitality across four continents, offering unparalleled service and enigmatic charm.

Founded by visionary leaders Pranav Kumar Singh and Ansh Singh, H.O.D combines architectural grandeur with modern sophistication, creating unforgettable experiences for discerning travelers worldwide.
